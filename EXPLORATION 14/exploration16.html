About Me

Hi, I’m Tanisha! A passionate home cook and food explorer. I started this blog to share my favorite recipes and my love for flavors with the world. Let’s cook, taste, and celebrate food together . Welcome to my café, a cozy little space born out of my passion for great coffee, meaningful connections, and creative design. As a UI-UX designer, I wanted to create more than just a place to grab a cup — it’s a warm, inspiring environment where every detail, from the menu to the atmosphere, reflects care and creativity. Here, you’ll find thoughtfully crafted drinks, fresh bites, and a vibe that feels like a second home. Whether you’re here to work, catch up with friends, or simply relax, my goal is to offer you a comfortable spot where you can pause, recharge, and enjoy the little joys of life.
